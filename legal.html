Document handling

Browser document handling

RewirePDF is designed primarily as a browser editor. Documents are opened and processed by the app in your browser. RewirePDF does not require accounts, a cloud document library, or automatic document uploads. The PWA cache is intended for the app shell, not for storing the PDFs you open.

The project website may be served through hosting infrastructure and normal browser/network requests. You should still avoid opening documents you are not authorized to use and should review exported PDFs before relying on them.
